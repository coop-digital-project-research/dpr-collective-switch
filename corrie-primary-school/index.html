Story

We’re raising money for new playground equipment by asking families to switch to a fantastic, small energy company that provides cheap, clean power.

When you switch, Corrie Primary School gets £25.

If 50 families pledge, we’ll be able to buy new slide for the playground.

If you've never switched before, green energy will be cheaper. It's a simple tariff that stays cheap year after year, unlike many of the big companies.

Going green — and saving money

We already have a team of eco warriors who help the school to be greener by turning lights off when the classroom is empty, encouraging paper recycling and litter picking in the school grounds.

This green switch will reduce our carbon footprint, equivalent to taking around 8 cars off the road.

It's in our power, together we can stop burning fossil fuels.
