Story

We’re raising money for the school library by asking families to switch to a fantastic, small energy company that provides cheap, clean power.

When you switch, Swinton Fitzwilliam gets £25.

If 60 families pledge, we’ll be able to buy 80 more books for the school library.

If you've never switched before, green energy will be cheaper and you'll never have to switch again.

Going green — and saving money

3 years ago we installed solar panels on the roof to power our school. These panels, along with our batteries, now save us £3,000 a year! They also mean Swinton Fitzwilliam now burns far less dirty fossil fuels.

This green switch will reduce our carbon footprint, equivalent to taking around 10 cars off the road.

It's in our power, together we can stop burning fossil fuels.
